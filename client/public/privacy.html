Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

Introduction

FreshTrade Distribution ("we", "our", "us") operates the FreshTrade Distribution Management System. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

When you connect your QuickBooks Online account, we access the following data:

  • Customer names and contact information
  • Invoice totals and payment status
  • Credit memo totals
  • Payment records
  • Payment terms

We do not access inventory data, line-item details, bank account information, or accounts payable data.

How We Use Your Information

Your QuickBooks data is used solely for the purpose of managing your distribution operations, including customer management, invoicing, and payment tracking within the FreshTrade system.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers. OAuth tokens used to connect to QuickBooks are encrypted and stored securely. We do not share, sell, or distribute your data to any third parties.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may disconnect from QuickBooks at any time, which will remove your stored OAuth tokens. You may request deletion of all your data by contacting us.

Third-Party Services

We integrate with Intuit QuickBooks Online to sync financial data. Intuit's privacy policy governs their handling of your data. We encourage you to review Intuit's Privacy Statement.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at the email address associated with your FreshTrade account.
